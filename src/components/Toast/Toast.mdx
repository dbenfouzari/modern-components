# Toast.Container

Use `Toast.Container` to position the `Toast`s on a side

<PropsTable of={Toast.Container} />

## Basic usage

You MUST use `Toast.Container` if you want the toasts to be positioned on a side

<Playground>
  <div>
    <span>See the toasts at top right position</span>
  
    <Toast.Container position='top-right'>
      <Toast>Hey ! I'm a toast</Toast>
      <Toast>I am too !</Toast>
      <Toast>Hey, don't give a fuck, <strong>I AM</strong> a toast Hey, don't give a fuck, I AM a toast</Toast>
    </Toast.Container>
  </div>
</Playground>
